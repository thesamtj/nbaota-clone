WALE OLUMIDE ADEGOKE, ACIArb, AICMC ESQ Incumbent Chairman, NBA OTA

Profile

LAWYER WALE OLUMIDE ADEGOKE hails from Ondo City in Ondo State. He completed his secondary education from Ondo Boys High School, 1992 as the best graduating student. He graduated from the fmr. Ondo State University [OSUA] in 1998 with second Class Upper and was called to the Nigerian Bar with honours at the Nigeria Law School, Bwari, Abuja in the year 2000.

LAWYER WALE OLUMIDE ADEGOKE completed his National Youth Service Programme [NYSC] in the Human Resources Department of the United Bank for Africa [UBA] in the year 2002. In the course of his service, he was a recipient of the Lagos State commendation award and the Co-ordinator of the Legal Clinic Unit of Lawyers Corp members serving in Lagos State.

LAWYER WALE OLUMIDE ADEGOKE in 2009 became a Chartered Arbitrator [ACIArb] with distinction as the overall best student. Afterwards, he has attended several continuing legal education programmes towards sharpening his intellectual and leadership skills. In 2022 he successfully completed a professional certification course programme with the Institute of Chartered Mediators and Conciliators [ICMC]

LAWYER WALE OLUMIDE ADEGOKE has worked with bank, reputable law firms and multinational Company as Legal Adviser. He is currently the Senior Partner of First Zenith Law Firm, an ever growing law firm with offices at No. 1 Unity Close, Lafenwa, Itele, Ota & Plot 24b Isheri Road, Ikeja, Lagos

LAWYER WALE OLUMIDE ADEGOKE in the course of his legal pursuits has enjoyed the rich mentorship of legal giants such as: Prof. Jelili Omotola, SAN, Chief Anthony Idigbe, SAN, and Chief Funsho Akinyosoye, SAN.

LAWYER WALE OLUMIDE ADEGOKE practice is focused on litigation and Corporate/ Commercial practices from the starting point of his career till date. He has continued to maintain his gaze on law and no no other vocation. He has also been involved in high profile cases with material impacts on the development of our law.

Leadership Roles

LAWYER WALE OLUMIDE is presently the Chairman of the Nigerian Bar Association, Ota Branch. He has also served in various leadership positions which includes Chairman of the Nigerian Bar Association, Human Right Committee, Member, Governing Council of Faith Academy Secondary school, Gowon Estate, Lagos & Kingdom Heritage Model School [KHMS] among others.

LAWYER WALE OLUMIDE ADEGOKE is a community leader, lover of humanity, consummate educationist and public interests advocate. He has served in various leadership positions which includes Chairman of the Nigerian Bar Association, Ota, Chairman of the Human Right Chambers, Electoral Chairman and on the Governing Council of Faith Academy Secondary school, Gowon Estate, Lagos & Kingdom Heritage Model School [KHMS] among others.

Pro-Bono Services

LAWYER WALE OLUMIDE ADEGOKE currently handles prosecution of rape cases under the aegis of a non - governmental institution called STER [Stand To End Rape] based in Lekki. He represents victims of abuse with a view to putting to an end the social stigma associated with rape by seeking justice for the survivor and the society.

Paper Presentations
