CHAIRMAN'S INAUGURAL SPEECH


ACCEPTANCE SPEECH BY ADEGOKE ADEWALE, ESQ., ACIAbr ‐ CHAIRMAN‐ELECT, NBA OTA BRANCH. JUNE 28, 2022

Distinguished Senior Advocates of Nigeria, O.O. OJUTALAYO, SAN & O.T. AKINBIYI, SAN , Elders of our Great Branch, Learned Seniors, and Honourable Colleagues, permit me to express my profound gratitude to God Almighty for this peaceful election.

My special thanks goes to the Almighty God and His Servant, BISHOP DAVID OYEDEPO for year 2022 ‘More than Conqueror’s Grace’ that has greatly impacted many lives including mine immensely.

I thank every eligible voter who took time out of their busy schedule to vote at the NBA Ota Branch election of today, June 28, 2022

Your determination and keen interest leading to your qualification to vote and your actual exercise of your voting obligation today is highly commendable. Words are not enough to convey my deep appreciation for the honour done to me and my team by this election. I cannot thank you enough.

I also appreciate all members of the Branch for the support, words of encouragement, and congratulatory messages in respect of this election.

Kudos to all elected members of the Executive Committee. As opined by my Chairman, J.T. OGUNNIYI in July, 2020, ‘this is a joint ticket’ . I look forward to a robust working relationship with you towards delivering maximally on our campaign promises. As you are aware, it is sacrosanct that we must deliver on the social contract we have with our members.

Once again, I indeed appreciate and celebrate all the outgoing Executive members, especially my Leader and Mentor, the Chairman, MR. J.T. OGUNNIYI for keeping the Branch together these past two years. I must also appreciate my brother, LEKAN DARAMOLA ESQ for his contributions.

I have learnt humility, love and maturity from the actions and unsaid words of our NOBLE CHAIRMAN - MR. J.T. OGUNNIYI , who has tremendously built monumental legacies after the revolutionary legacies of our Chairman Emeritus, ISAAC OMUTA OGBAH . We wish each and every outgoing Executive member the best in their future endeavours. We will surely consolidate on the legacies of our heroes and past administrations in taking the ‘UTTER BAR’ to the next levels of achievements with God on our side.

I specially commend Learned Seniors, S.A. AMEH, OTUNBA OLUMIDE AKINBINU, ALHAJI T.G. ADEOSUN, AKIN FOLARIN ESQ and ALHAJI ALLI DAOUDU . for their sagacious words of encouragement. I also commend our Chairman Emeritus, ISAAC OMUTA OGBAH ESQ . for his profound support. In addition, I commend our Emeritus Secretary, O.A. PIUS ESQ . for his lavish positive influences.

I commend all bar leaders particularly CHIEF IBUKUN OREMODU, IPADE AWOWOLO ESQ., MONZOR OWOYEMI ESQ., AKIN SANNI ESQ. and ABRAHAM ADEOYE ESQ. for their supports. I cannot repay the debt of gratitude I owe you all.

I once again thank everyone who contributed in one way or the other to the success we are celebrating today. I must appreciate my Associate in Chamber, OGORI NNENNA [MRS.] for holding forte in my absence. I also remember my Sister, CHIEF AJOSE KELUBIA for her solidarity. Worthy of mention are members of the Human Rights/Probono Committee who provided the fulcrum for the success of today. It is my prayer that God Almighty will reward everyone a zillion fold.

I must commend members of the Branch Electoral Committee for a job well done despite daunting challenges. I commend the Chairperson, R.E. ITUA [MRS.], KAREEM KELANI ESQ., ODUNFA ADEDAPO ESQ. & MISS. ADENIKE ADELEKE for their wisdom and dexterity in delivering the election without strife in our Branch.

Let me seize this opportunity to state that this Branch belongs to all of us. The welfare of members is of paramount importance to us and we shall deliver on this covenant. Our ISAAC OMUTA OGBAH candidature for the post of 3rd Vice President, NBA National is a Branch project. I urge us all to drum up support for him at all fora. We pledge to do all within our powers as Executives Elect to deliver him come the 16th of July, 2022. May God help us all. Amen!

We expect everyone of us to contribute his/her quota to the growth and development of the Branch by participating in different committees and programmes of the Branch towards raising the luster of our Branch in the comity of NBA branches and repositioning our Branch as the Branch to beat as we take steps to usher in greater impacts for our members and the Branch.

On our part, we promise to uphold the Nigerian Bar Association Constitution, enabling Bye-laws and implement programmes that will bring massive developmental impacts to the affairs of the Branch.

For the records our Vision is to build a ‘Greater Utter Bar’ [GUT] and doing this we shall focus on the following objectives among others:

  1. Completion and furnishing of the Bar Centre
  2. Setting up an affordable Lawyer’s Estate
  3. Acquisition of the Utter Bar Bus to consolidate on our Brand identity;
  4. Setting up an e-voting platform for the next elections for flexibility and ease of voting;
  5. To bring in corporate sponsorship towards broadening our branch income base;
  6. Setting up framework for lawyers and principals to key into the Contributory Pension Scheme; and
  7. Ensuring continuity of our annual law week with colour and panache.

We will also leverage on the excellent work by the outgoing administration and continue to do the followings:

  1. Ensure that the Unity of the Utter Bar is maintained at all Times;
  2. Ensure the Elders are galvanized to attend meetings;
  3. Strive to substantially increase the membership strength of the Branch by encouraging more lawyers to join the Branch;
  4. Ensure the welfare of members of the Utter Bar by continuing the Insurance Scheme of the Branch and possibly renegotiate terms that would be more favorable to members;
  5. Strive to ensure that members attendance to Monthly meetings are on steady increase;
  6. Also ensure that Continuing Legal Education through periodic lectures becomes a yearly affair;
  7. Strengthening the bond between the Bar and the Bench through the Bar and Bench Forum.

God bless us all.

Long live NBA Ota Branch!

Long live Nigerian Bar Association!

Long live the Federal Republic of Nigeria!

Dated Tuesday, the 28th day of June, 2022.

ADEGOKE, A.O. ESQ. ACIAbr (Incumbent Chairmain, NBA, OTA)
