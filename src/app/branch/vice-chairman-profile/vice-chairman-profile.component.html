AYODEJI ISIOLAOTAN, AICMC. Incumbent Vice Chairman, NBA OTA

Profile

Ayodeji Isiolaotan is a native of Ikeji Ile Ijesa in Osun State.

Attended Ikeji Ile High School and Saint Peter's Unity Secondary School, Akure, Ondo State.

A graduate of the University of Ibadan and called to the Nigerian Bar in the year 2007.

Vice Chairman NBA Ota.

Legal Adviser, NBA Ota, 2020-2022.

Chairman Continuing Legal Education Committee 2020-2022. Bar Activities!

Ayodeji Isiolaotan has served, and he is still serving in of the Nigerian Bar Association.

He was the Legal Adviser of Nigerian Bar Association, Ota Branch from 2020-2022.

He was also the Chaiman, Continuing Legal Education Committee, Nigerian Bar Association Ota from 2020-2022 and under his leadership, the Committee organised lectures for the benefits of the members of the Bar and Society.

He also served as members of various committees of the Bar few, amongst which are Data and Identity Card Committee Law Week Planning Committee and Website Committee.

He is the incumbent Vice Chairman of Nigerian Bar Association, Ota.

Pro Bono:

Ayodeji Isiolaotan has always rendering pro bono services as far back as the year 2011.

By virtue of his position as the Vice Chairman, NBA Ota who is the statutory chairman of Human Rights Committee, he is engaged in some pro bono cases in Court.

He is happily married with children!
