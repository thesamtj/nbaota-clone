WALE OLUMIDE ADEGOKE, ACIArb Incumbent Vice Chairman, NBA OTA

Profile

WALE OLUMIDE ADEGOKE [WAO] is a detribalized, courageous Nigerian lawyer who graduated from the fmr. Ondo State University [OSUA] in 1998 and was called to the Nigerian Bar in the year 2000 at the Nigeria Law School, Bwari, Abuja.

In 2009, he became a Chartered Arbitrator [ACIArb] with distinction as the overall best student. Afterwards, he has attended several continuing legal education programmes towards sharpening his intellectual and leadership skills.

The candidate has worked with bank, reputable law firms and multinational Company as Legal Adviser. He is currently the Senior Partner of First Zenith Law Firm, an ever growing law firm with office at No. 1 Unity Close, Lafenwa, Itele, Ota.

WALE OLUMIDE ADEGOKE [WAO] in the course of his legal pursuits has enjoyed the rich mentorship of legal giants such as: Prof. Jelili Omotola, SAN, Chief Anthony Idigbe, SAN, and Chief Funsho Akinyosoye, SAN.

His practice is focused on litigation and Corporate/ Commercial practices from the starting point of his career till date. He has continued to maintained his gaze on law and no his gaze on law and no other vocation. He has also been involved in high profile cases with material impacts on the development of our law.

Leadership Roles

Wale Olumide Adegoke [WAO] is a community leader, lover of humanity, consummate educationist and public interests advocate. He has served in various leadership positions which includes Chairman of the Human Right Chambers, Electoral Chairman and on the Governing Council of Faith Academy Secondary school, Gowon Estate, Lagos & Kingdom Heritage Model School [KHMS] among others.

Bar Activities

Wale Olumide Adegoke [WAO] was previously member of Lagos and Ikeja Bar as Corporate Counsel prior to joining the UTTER BAR some years ago. He has served the UTTER BAR in the Law Week and Human Right Committees. He is also part of the team prosecuting fake lawyers within the jurisdiction.

Pro-Bono Services

Wale Olumide Adegoke [WAO] currently handles prosecution of rape cases under the aegis of a non - governmental institution called STER [Stand To End Rape] based in Lekki. He represents victims of abuse with a view to putting to an end the social stigma associated with rape by seeking justice for the survivor and the society.

Paper Presentations

In the course of his career development, he represented Chief Anthony Idigbe, SAN at the ivory of Nigeria legal education, the NIGERIAN INSTITUTE OF LEGAL STUDIES, UNILAG, AKOKA, LAGOS as a lead speaker and presented a paper on the topic of –‘Legal & Commercial Imports of the Concept of Mareva Injunction’as a continuing legal education for legal practitioners, judges and legal administrators from all over the country. Sometimes in March, 2019, he was also invited by the Management of Covenant University to present a paper on ‘Cybercrimes under the Nigerian Law which was a huge success.

Family Life

He is happily married to Mrs. Treasure Adegoke and blessed with children.
