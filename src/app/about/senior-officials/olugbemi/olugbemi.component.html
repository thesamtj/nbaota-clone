HON. JUSTICE SOLOMON ABIDOYE OLUGBEMI, JP (RTD) Fmr. Judge, Ogun State

Abridged Profile

Honourable Justice Solomon Abidoye Olugbemi (Rtd) hails from Abeokuta, Ogun State of Nigeria. He attended Ogbomoso Grammar School, Ogbomoso, Oyo State; Yaba College of Technology, Yaba, Lagos; University of Lagos, Akoka, Lagos; Nigerian Law School, Victoria Island, Lagos; Lagos State University, Ojo, Lagos; and the Nigerian Baptist Theological Seminary, Ogbomoso, Nigeria.

Hon. Justice Olugbemi has a Diploma in Secretarial Studies, Bachelor of Laws, Master of Laws (Maritime and Commercial Law) and Master of Divinity. He was called to the Nigerian Bar in July 1983. He served in the National Youth Service Corps in 1983/1984. He was made a Notary Public for Nigeria in February 1990.

Honourable Justice Olugbemi practised as a private Legal Practitioner in Nigeria for twenty five years. He is a former Chairman of the Nigerian Bar Association, Ota Branch. He was appointed a Judge of the Ogun State High Court in July 2008 and he retired on 2nd April 2020.

Hon. Justice Olugbemi is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Mediators and Conciliators, a Member of the Dispute Resolution International and an Associate of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (UK). He is a Minister of the Gospel of Christ. He is now engaged in Mediation, Arbitration, Legal Consultancy and Publishing. He has published many legal and Christian articles, tracts and a book namely:

  • Res Ipsa Loquitor or Confusion in the Proof of Negligence? (The Lawyer, University of Lagos, 1982)
  • Workplace Ambassadors, a lecture delivered at the First Quarter Fellowship of the Goodnews Baptist Association, Lagos held at the Wisdom Assembly, Idimu, Lagos on 3rd of March, 2018.
  • Order of Certiorari: Its Use and Limitations, published in “Essays in Honour of Honourable Justice Olatokunbo O. Olopade, CON, KJW (Ogun State Bar and Bench Forum, Abeokuta, July, 2018).
  • The Church, the State and the Rule of Law, paper delivered at the Annual Retreat of the Ogun State Baptist Pastors Fellowship on 7th November 2018.
  • Tracts – ‘First Things First’ and ‘Escape for Your Life!’
  • Driving a Positive Change, A Leadership Role Within a Community, a paper delivered at “A Night-out with Oluomo, a forum of Apomu Community, Ogun State on 23rd November 2018.
  • Book: Sweet Memories on the Bench, Vol. 1, 2nd April 2020.

Hon. Justice Olugbemi has travelled to most geo-political zones of Nigeria. He has also travelled to some parts of the world including Kenya, United States of America, the United Kingdom, the UAE and Israel. He speaks English and Yoruba fluently and Hausa and Igbo to some extent.

Hon. Justice Olugbemi is married with children and grandchildren and he now lives in Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria.
