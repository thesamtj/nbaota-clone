ELDER (DR) SIMON A. AMEH Principal Partner @ Ameh, Ameh & Co.

Short Profile

The Amiable, cheerful, detribalized, tolerant, youthful and zealous ELDER (DR) SIMON A. AMEH, ACIArb. U.K was born at INYE in Ankpa Local Government Area of Kogi State to Late Mr. & Mrs Ademu Ameh over Fifty years ago.

Education

ELDER (DR) SIMON A. AMEH had his Nursery Education at CRI Nursery School, INYE in 1973, his Primary Education at LGED Primary School, Inye where he obtained Certificate of Primary Education in 1979. He then proceeded to Inye Community Secondary School, INYE-Ankpa in Kogi State where he emerged as the best graduating student in 1984. He got his General Certificate of Education, GCE “O” L in 1984. He also attended the School of Basic Studies, Makurdi an obtained his IJMB Certificate in 1986 and proceeded to read Law at the University of Jos and obtained his L.L.B (Hons) in 1989 and thereafter to the Nigeria Law School, Lagos where he got Barrister at Law (B.L) Certificate in 1990 and called to the Nigerian Bar on 12/12/1990. He Obtained his National Youth Service Corps Certificate in 1991.

As a youth corps member, he was first posted to the office of the Secretary to the Ogun State Government, Abeokuta but after 3 weeks assignment, his enthusiasm and passion for legal practice drove him to seek for redeployment to the law firm of Olumuyiwa Akinsanya & Co. at Sagamu, Ogun State and same was granted. The law firm was later re-named Akinsanya, Ameh & Co. and headed by the Late Otunba Dan Ameh (of the blessed memory). Elder (DR) S. A. Ameh was retained as a Junior Partner for his credulous and worthy contribution to the firm. In 1993, a branch office of the law firm was opened at Ota, Ogun State and he was posted to Head the Branch. In 1995, the name of the law firm was changed to Ameh, Ameh & Co. and he is presently the Head of Chambers, with offices at Sagamu, Ota in Ogun State and Lokoja in Kogi State.

He is an active member of the Nigeria Bar Association. He served the Ota Branch in many capacities, ranging from Financial Secretary, Treasurer, Vice Chairman and Chairman respectively. He served in many committees both at the National and local levels of the NBA which include the NEC Membership, Chairman, Disciplinary Committee, Caretaker Committee, Election Committee at various times and officiated as the Chairman Electoral Committee in the just conducted election of the NBA Ota Branch. He has attended all Annual General Conferences of the NBA from 1992 till date.

He is a Chartered Arbitrator and Mediator and has numerous awards in recognition of his proficient, excellent and exemplary contributions to the legal practice and humanity including the NBA award of Excellence in 2018.

He has mentored many lawyers who are also successful today in their fields of endeavour.

He was ordained as an ELDER of Qua Iboe Church now United Evangelical Church of Nigeria in 2004 and became one of the National Legal Advisers from 2014 till date. He is currently the Chairman, Church Committee of the Sango-Ota Branch of UEC.

He was conferred with an award of a Doctorate Degree in Divinity in 2013.

He is of the Royalty, the Onu-Igala 1 of Ogun State, a Community Leader, Lover of humanity and public interest advocate.

ELDER (DR) SIMON A. AMEH is an embodiment of humility and a model, worthy of emulation. An intelligent, vibrant and an erudite Lawyer of outstanding resilience, a staunch, loyal and devoted Man of God.

He is happily married with children.
