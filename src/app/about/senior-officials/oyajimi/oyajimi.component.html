HON. JUSTICE OLATUNDE HASSAN OYAJINMI Judge, Ogun State

Profile

Hon Justice Olatunde Hassan Oyajinmi, an indigene of Agosasa, Ipokia Local Government Area, Ogun State is a twin and the 3rd child of Late Alhaji(Chief) S.O Oyajinmi, who until his death was a renowned educationist and the Balogun Musulumi of the Agosasa Muslim Community and Alhaja S.I Oyajinmi a retiree attended Community High School, Agosasa, Ogun State for his secondary school education and he is an alumnus of the Faculty of Law, Ogun State University now Olabisi Onabanjo University Ago-Iwoye.

After his call to the Nigerian Bar and while remaining in active private legal practice, Hon Justice Oyajinmi was appointed a Notary Public of the Supreme Court of Nigeria by the Chief Justice of Nigeria in 2007.

Until his appointment as a judge of the High Court of Ogun State in November, 2018, his lordship established and was the principal solicitor of AL-ADL Law Firm, a firm that was involved as solicitors and advocate in diverse areas of law including but not limited to litigations at the High Courts and the Appellate Courts after his pupillage in the chambers of Opaleye, Gbadebo & Co.

His lordship was an active bar man who served the Nigerian Bar Association as Assistant Branch Secretary between 2005-2007 and Branch Secretary, Ota first between 2007-2009 which position enabled him to serve as a member of Nigerian Bar Association National Executive Council(NEC), the decision making organ of the Nigerian Bar Association(NBA) in Nigeria and his lordship also served again as Branch Secretary of the Nigerian Bar Association, Ota branch between 2016-2018 which position he relinquished in July 2018 after the statutory tour of service.

He is a Chartered Arbitrator and Mediator and has numerous awards in recognition of his proficient, excellent and exemplary contributions to the legal practice and humanity including the NBA award of Excellence in 2018.

Hon Justice Oyajinmi, a member of Nigerian Institute of Mediation and Conciliations (N.I.M.C) is a devout moslem and family man.

He is also a multiple Paul Harris Fellow of the Rotary International and served as the 25th President of the Rotary Club of Ota in the 2007\2008 Rotary year.
